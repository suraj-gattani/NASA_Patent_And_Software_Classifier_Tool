An adaptation of Microsoft Excel and ASPEN (Activity Scheduling/Planning Environment) for Lunar Surface System concepts of operations. The goal of the overall system is to enable searching through several concepts of operations, where a concept of operations consists of a proposed schedule of high-level activities and parameterization of resources (e.g., power, communications, oxygen, water, etc.) three distinct phases of development occurred: 1) initial system development for Lunar, 2) planning system development for Lunar, and 3) planning system development for NASAs thirteenth Desert Research and Technology Studies (Desert RATS) live trial.
