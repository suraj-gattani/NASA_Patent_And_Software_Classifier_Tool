Augmented reality application intended to highlight NASA Glenn's research efforts in the area of flywheel energy storage systems. The app utilizes a printed optical target, available on the GRC Flywheel Program brochure and through the NASA Glenn web portal, to present users with 3D views of flywheel hardware and space applications. Additional video and imagery are also inlcuded in the application package. The software has been compiled and bundled as an iOS app for the iPad and intended for release through the Apple App Store.
